Siddharth Mehra

About

Siddharth Mehra is a dedicated philosopher, research scholar, and education reformer hailing from Ajmer, Rajasthan. With a profound academic foundation, He completed his Master’s in Philosophy in 2022, achieving 1st rank in his academic program, and was honoured with a merit award from MDS University for his outstanding performance. and earned distinction by securing the 1st rank in motivational writing and 2nd rank overall in his post-graduate program at SPCGC, Ajmer. In 2024, he advanced his scholarly journey by completing a Ph.D. in Philosophy from the Department of Sanskrit, Philosophy, and Vedic Studies at Banasthali Vidyapith, where his research interweaves Indian philosophical traditions with contemporary discourse.
Siddharth has authored numerous national and international research articles covering topics like Sanskrit literature, Indian logic, and education systems. He has actively participated in interdisciplinary conferences and seminars, presenting on themes such as education as a socio-political tool, world peace and consciousness, and the role of language and literature in mental health. His work often bridges traditional wisdom with modern philosophical concerns.
He is also the founder of the Bhavishyasetu Foundation, an NGO committed to transforming the future of Bharat through holistic education and youth empowerment. His efforts reflect a strong commitment to cultural integrity, educational innovation, and philosophical exploration.
Recognized for his academic excellence and intellectual contributions, Siddharth continues to explore how ancient Indian texts such as the Ramcharitmanas, Valmiki Ramayana, and Sanskrit scriptures can illuminate ethical, spiritual, and scientific dimensions of modern life.
